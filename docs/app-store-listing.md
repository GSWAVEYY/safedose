### Full Description
*(4000 character limit)*

```
SafeDose helps you manage your medications safely — checking for drug interactions, sending reminders, and letting your caregiver know you're on track. All without your health data ever leaving your phone.

For informational purposes only. Always consult your doctor or pharmacist before making any medication changes.

---

HOW IT WORKS

Add your medications by scanning the pill bottle with your camera or typing the name. SafeDose checks each medication against the others you're taking using a drug interaction database stored directly on your device. No internet required for interaction checks.

---

DRUG INTERACTION INFORMATION

SafeDose uses drug interaction data from the National Institutes of Health (RxNorm and NDF-RT) — the same public sources pharmacists reference. When you add a new medication, SafeDose checks it against your entire list and flags known interactions so you can discuss them with your healthcare provider.

SafeDose provides this information for reference only. It is not a substitute for professional advice from your doctor or pharmacist.

---

MEDICATION REMINDERS

Set reminders for each medication on whatever schedule you need — daily, twice daily, weekly. SafeDose tracks whether you've confirmed each dose so you always know where you stand. No more wondering "did I already take that?"

---

CAREGIVER LINKING (Premium)

Invite a family member to see your medication adherence. Your caregiver gets notified when you confirm your doses and can see if you've missed one. They see adherence data only — never your private notes or medical details.

---

EMERGENCY INFO CARD

Create a QR code with your medications, allergies, and emergency contacts. First responders can scan it instantly. No app needed on their end.

---

PRIVACY BY DESIGN

Your medication data belongs to you:
• Drug interaction checks happen on your device — no data sent to any server
• Medication data is encrypted and stored on your phone
• Sync (Premium): your data is encrypted on your device before it ever reaches our server — we cannot read it
• We never sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it to build profiles

---

DESIGNED FOR REAL USE

We built SafeDose for people managing 8, 10, or 12 medications a day — not for people managing two vitamins. That means:
• Large, easy-to-read text throughout
• VoiceOver and Dynamic Type supported
• High contrast display option
• Large touch targets throughout
• Camera scanning for users who find typing difficult
```

## Apple Review Preparation Notes

### Review Q&A

**"Does this app diagnose medical conditions?"**
No. SafeDose tracks medications and checks for known interactions using public NIH databases (RxNorm, NDF-RT). It does not diagnose, recommend treatment, or suggest dosage changes. The user makes all decisions. Every interaction alert includes a prompt to consult a pharmacist or doctor.

**"What are your sources for drug interaction data?"**
RxNorm (National Library of Medicine, NIH) and NDF-RT (Veterans Administration / NLM). Both are public domain. No licensing required.

**"How is user health data handled?"**
Free tier: medication data stored on-device only. No server sync. Premium tier: data is encrypted on-device with AES-256-GCM before transmission. The server stores ciphertext only. Zero-knowledge architecture — the server cannot read medication data.

**Review submission note to include:**
> SafeDose is a medication management tool that uses public FDA databases (RxNorm, NDF-RT) for interaction information. It does not diagnose, treat, or prescribe. All interaction alerts include a disclaimer directing users to consult their pharmacist. All medication data stays on-device (free tier) or is encrypted before any sync (premium tier). Demo account available upon request.
