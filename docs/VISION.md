# SafeDose — Vision & Mission

**A VRAXON Digital Product**
**Last updated:** March 2026

---

## Mission

Make medication management safe, simple, and accessible — especially for the 53 million unpaid caregivers who have zero purpose-built tools.

## The Problem We Solve

Medication errors are the 4th leading cause of death in America:

- **125,000 Americans die annually** from medication errors and adverse drug interactions
- **53 million Americans** are unpaid caregivers with no purpose-built tools
- **Doctors don't always know** what other doctors prescribed. Pharmacists catch some interactions, miss others
- **Elderly managing 8-12 meds daily** have no safety net beyond a pill organizer
- **Adult children caring for parents remotely** have zero visibility into whether mom took her meds
- **40% of Alzheimer's caregivers** die before the person they care for — from stress and burnout
- **Rural communities and non-English speakers** are even more underserved

## What SafeDose Does

Entry point: medication management + drug interaction checking.
Expands to: full caregiver coordination platform.

Scan a pill bottle, and SafeDose identifies the medication, checks it against everything else you're taking, sets up reminders, and lets your caregiver see that you're on track — all without your health data ever leaving your device.

**One sentence:** The pharmacist in your pocket, for patients and the people who care for them.

## Who It's For

| Audience | Why They Need It |
|----------|-----------------|
| **Elderly patients (65+)** | Managing 8-12 meds daily. Can't read small print. Forget doses. |
| **Adult children (35-55)** | Caring for aging parents, often remotely. Need visibility without micromanaging. |
| **Unpaid caregivers (53M)** | Juggling medications, appointments, and their own lives. Zero tools built for them. |
| **Chronic illness patients** | Multiple specialists = multiple prescriptions = interaction risk |
| **Non-English speakers** | Medication labels in English only. Language barrier = safety gap. |

## What Makes SafeDose Different

1. **On-device drug interaction checking** — No cloud dependency. Works offline. FDA data bundled locally.
2. **Camera OCR scanning** — Point at a pill bottle, auto-populate medication details
3. **Zero-knowledge sync** — Server never sees plaintext medication data. AES-256-GCM encryption.
4. **Caregiver linking** — Family members monitor medication adherence without seeing private health details
5. **Emergency info card** — QR code with medications, allergies, emergency contacts — accessible by first responders
6. **Free core** — Basic medication management + interaction checking costs nothing

## Brand Identity

**"Calm Precision"** — clinical accuracy with human warmth. NOT sterile hospital aesthetic.

SafeDose looks like it was designed by a pharmacist who genuinely cares about you. Clean whites, structured layouts, legible type — but warm. Red means one thing: contraindicated drug interaction. If red appears everywhere, it means nothing.

**Tagline:** "Know before you take."

**Tone:** Knowledgeable, calm, precise, on your side.
